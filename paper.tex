\title{Health and Safety Systems in the Oil and Gas Industry Post-COVID}

\author{\IEEEauthorblockN{Osheen, Manu Garg}}

\begin{document}

\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
This research analyzes the transformation of Health and Safety Management Systems (HSMS) in the oil and gas industry after COVID-19. It highlights challenges such as workforce fragmentation, remote monitoring, and digital transformation.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
The COVID-19 pandemic disrupted traditional safety systems and forced organizations to adopt remote monitoring and digital technologies.

\section{Methodology}
The study uses qualitative analysis including interviews, surveys, and document review.

\section{Results and Findings}
Key findings include the emergence of digital paradox, increased safety awareness, and challenges in maintaining workforce well-being.

\section{Conclusion}
Future HSMS must balance technology with human decision-making for better safety outcomes.
