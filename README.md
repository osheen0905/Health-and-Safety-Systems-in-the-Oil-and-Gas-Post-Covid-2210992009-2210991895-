# Health-and-Safety-Systems-in-the-Oil-and-Gas-Post-Covid-2210992009-2210991895-
This research analyzes how Health and Safety Management Systems (HSMS) in the oil and gas industry evolved post-COVID. It highlights challenges like remote operations, workforce fragmentation, and digital overload, and proposes a resilient, human-centric safety framework combining technology, leadership, and workforce engagement.
